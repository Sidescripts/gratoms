Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on gratoms?

Creating an account is simple. Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner, provide your email address, create a password, and verify your email. Once verified, you can complete your profile and start trading.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including popular cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin and Ethereum. Deposit options may vary based on your region.

Is gratoms available in my country?

gratoms is available in over 150 countries worldwide. However, some services may be limited in certain regions. Check our availability page for specific information about your country.

How secure is my funds and personal information?

We employ bank-level security measures including SSL encryption, two-factor authentication, and cold storage for cryptocurrencies. Additionally, we regularly undergo security audits to ensure the highest level of protection for your assets and data.

Do you offer mobile trading apps?

We’re working on bringing you a fully-featured mobile app for both iOS and Android. Soon, you’ll be able to access the same powerful trading tools from our web platform right on your phone; so you can trade anywhere, anytime.
